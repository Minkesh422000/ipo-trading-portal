## Scoring Framework

| Dimension | Weight | Criteria |
|-----------|--------|----------|
| Valuation vs peers | 20% | P/E vs sector median (~72x): <40x=5, 40–60x=4, 60–80x=3, 80–100x=2, >100x=1; loss-making=0 |
| Revenue CAGR (3yr) | 20% | >20%=5, 15–20%=4, 10–15%=3, 5–10%=2, <5%=1 |
| Order book strength | 25% | Book-to-bill: >3x=5, 2–3x=4, 1.5–2x=3, 1–1.5x=2, <1x or N/A=2 |
| Quality (ROCE) | 20% | >25%=5, 20–25%=4, 15–20%=3, 10–15%=2, <10%=1 |
| Moat / policy tailwind | 15% | 1–5 subjective: proprietary products, captive govt contracts, export potential |

**Score = weighted sum × 20 (out of 100)**

## Company Mini-Profiles

### 1. MAZDOCK — Mazagon Dock Shipbuilders ⭐ TOP PICK
- **Business:** Only Indian shipyard building P75 Scorpene submarines and Project 17A stealth frigates
- **Financials:** Rev ₹12,330 Cr | ROCE 48.8% | ROE 36.5% | Zero debt
- **Order Book:** ~₹50,000 Cr | B2B ~4.1x | Revenue visibility: 4+ years
- **Thesis:** Irreplaceable monopoly in submarine construction; P75I (6 submarines, ~₹43,000 Cr) pending; Navy's 30-year shipbuilding plan ensures multi-decade runway
- **Risks:** Execution delays on complex naval programs; PSU bureaucracy; single customer (Indian Navy)

### 3. HAL — Hindustan Aeronautics Ltd ⭐ TOP PICK
- **Business:** State-owned aircraft OEM — Tejas fighters, Dhruv helicopters, aero engines, MRO
- **Financials:** Rev ₹32,846 Cr | ROCE 33.9% | ROE 26% | Zero debt | P/E 32.6x (cheapest quality name)
- **Order Book:** ₹2,54,000 Cr | B2B ~7.7x | 7+ years of revenue visibility

## Macro Backdrop

### Defense Budget Tailwinds
- India defense budget FY26: ~₹6.81 lakh Cr (+9.5% YoY); capital outlay ₹1.80 lakh Cr
- Target: 25% of defense procurement to be domestic by 2025 (up from ~65% already achieved)
- Positive Indigenisation List: 509 items banned from import — direct revenue for HAL, BEL, BDL, GRSE, MAZDOCK

### Policy Catalysts
- **Atmanirbhar Defence**: 68% defense budget ring-fenced for domestic industry
- **PLI Scheme**: ₹1,000 Cr incentives for aerospace components; benefits AEQUS, UNIMECH, AZAD
- **iDEX**: 300+ innovations funded; pipeline for DATAPATTNS, CENTUM, PARAS
- **Export Target**: ₹35,000 Cr by FY25 (India achieved ~₹21,000 Cr in FY24); SOLARINDS, BEL leading

### Key Risks to the Sector
1. Defense budget compression if fiscal deficit widens
2. PSU execution delays (HAL Tejas backlog)
3. China border de-escalation reducing urgency
4. Private sector competition squeezing PSU margins over 5–10 years
